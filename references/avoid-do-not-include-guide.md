# Avoid / Do Not Include Guide

Write a separate `Avoid / Do not include` list for each image. Tailor it to the subject type and visible failure risks. This section is the image-2-facing negative constraint area, but use the exact label `Avoid / Do not include` by default.

## Universal

`low quality, blurry details, overexposed highlights, underexposed shadows, bad composition, watermark, unwanted text, unwanted logo, jpeg artifacts, duplicate subject, poor lighting, unrealistic details, distorted perspective, unnatural texture, incorrect proportions, messy background`

## People And Portraits

Use when people are visible:

`bad anatomy, distorted face, extra fingers, missing fingers, deformed hands, stiff pose, lifeless expression, crossed eyes, asymmetrical eyes, unnatural pupils, deformed iris, bad proportions, plastic skin, over-smoothed face, dead eyes, awkward body posture, incorrect head angle, unnatural facial expression, wrong gaze direction, stiff fingers, broken wrist, unnatural shoulder position`

Add image-specific constraints when needed:

- If hands are prominent: emphasize `deformed hands, extra fingers, stiff fingers, broken wrist`.
- If gaze is important: emphasize `wrong gaze direction, crossed eyes, unnatural pupils, deformed iris`.
- If relaxed posture is important: emphasize `stiff pose, awkward body posture, unnatural shoulder position`.
- If skin texture matters: emphasize `plastic skin, over-smoothed face, unnatural skin texture`.

## Products

`deformed product, wrong proportions, distorted logo, unreadable text, messy reflection, low quality, bad lighting, warped edges, incorrect material, floating artifacts, wrong perspective, inconsistent shadows, overexposed product highlights`

## Architecture And Interiors

`warped perspective, distorted structure, impossible geometry, tilted lines, low detail, broken symmetry, unrealistic scale, messy architecture, incorrect vanishing point, bent vertical lines`

## Food

`unappetizing texture, messy composition, artificial colors, overprocessed details, low quality, unnatural gloss, dirty plate, fake food texture, wrong scale, plastic-looking food`

## Landscape

`oversaturated colors, messy composition, low resolution, unnatural lighting, blurry details, fake sky, overprocessed HDR, chaotic background, unnatural horizon line, distorted landscape`

## Writing Rule

Keep the list concise and targeted. Use compact English comma-separated terms, and avoid including constraints that would fight the visible target image.
